EndUser

Research and development for new drugs and diagnostics need human biological samples (HBS) of multi ethnic origin. Due to sparse biobanks and insufficient samples of ethically collected, curated human biological samples, the Indian ethnic population is not well represented in the safety and efficacy testing in the research projects. 64 Codon provides ethically collected and curated human biological samples from the multi ethnic Indian population. We also produce value-added products like - tissue micro array, DNA micro array, annotated images, whole slide images, FFPE with/without IHC, molecular data, etc. to cater to the researcher’s custom requirements. We welcome researchers, to contact us with your requirements.
