Modern research into the development of new drugs, diagnostics methods and insights into diseases requires human biological samples from a diverse multiethnic population pool. Unfortunately, Indian ethnic population is underrepresented due to lack of human biological samples of Indian origin, compounded by relative lack of biobanks. At 64 CODON, we endeavor to support the research community, academia and pharmaceutical industry by providing ethically collected and curated high quality human biological samples from our network spread across India.

At 64 Codon, we engage with hospitals and laboratories across the country to collect human biological samples of multi ethnic Indian origin. Samples are collected after executing MoUs and Agreements, ensuring anonymization of the samples, using proper tissue handling protocols and stored after quality control and curation. All the steps are carried out as per Ethical Committee guidelines / approvals, and as per the New Drugs and Clinical Trials Rules of 2019 and the ICMR guidelines. Prospective sample collection is carried out with comprehensive Informed Consent from patients, who have the right to withdraw consent at any point of time. A certain portion of the stored human biological samples may be made available on free of cost basis for conducting collaborative basic science research.

We welcome Clinicians, Hospitals, Laboratories and Patients, to connect with us and find out how YOU can contribute to Healthcare Research, by donating your samples.
