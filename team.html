Team

Our vibrant team consists of -

Our Technical Team are -

Dr. Malini Venkata – Pathologist

Dr. Malini is a medical professional with over 18 years’ experience in the field of pathology. She has broad spectrum professional experience, from academia to industry. Her areas of interest include digital pathology and biobanking. In her previous role at Bristol Myers Squibb in Bangalore, she was instrumental in setting up a state-of-the-art immunohistochemistry and digital pathology lab. She also played a key role in external alliance management, wherein she worked on setting up collaborations with specialty hospitals and driving translational research.




Dr. Durga Prasan – Oncologist

Dr. Durga Prasan is a senior consultant in medical oncology with over 15 years of experience. He has served as faculty in several large comprehensive cancer centres and teaching hospitals, and has helped establish medical oncology departments which have grown to be institutes of eminence. He has been a teacher and mentor in his field, and is currently pursuing his goal of being a clinician scientist. He graduated from Madras Medical College with MBBS, did his MD in Medicine from Aligarh Muslim University, and received his Diplomate of National Board in Medical Oncology after training at Dharamshila Cancer Hospital and All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi. He has professional certificate in Genetics and Genomics from Stanford University, and has been a recipient of DBT scholarship from Government of India. His research interests are in ovarian cancer and stem cell biology of cancers.




Dr. Raghunath Narayanan Unni – Pathologist

Dr. Raghunath completed post-graduation in Pathology from Kasturba Medical College, Manipal. He has worked as Assistant Professor of Pathology in Amala Institute of Medical Science, Thrissur; for 3 years before joining as Director in RP MedHelix Diagnostics Pvt Ltd. (an associate of 64 Codon), a specialist diagnostics laboratory providing histopathology services across Kerala. Dr. Raghunath is leading a team of pathologists and has gained significant experience in handling anatomic pathology, especially oncopathology, cases. He is also an International Society of Nephrology certified Nephropathologist.
