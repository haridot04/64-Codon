Services

Research and development for new drugs and diagnostics need human biological samples (HBS) of multi ethnic origin. Global Researchers work hard in translating the research innovations, products and services, to benefit larger communities. Due to sparse biobanks and insufficient samples of ethically collected, curated human biological samples, the Indian ethnic population is not well represented in the safety and efficacy testing in the research projects. 64 Codon aims to be the market place for tissue from the multi-ethnic Asian Indian populations. We are connected in an open network to hospitals and laboratories, and growing the number and varieties of HBS which are ethically collected, anonymized, curated and stored for use by researchers. Our services are cater to the researcher's custom requirements.

We offer:

• Ethically collected and curated human biological samples from the multi ethnic Indian population

• Value added products : Tissue microarray, DNA microarray, FFPE with/without IHC, Molecular data,etc.

• Digital pathology services
