About 64 Codon

64 Codon (formerly known as Greenle Life Sciences Pvt. Ltd.) is a bioscience company with an open network commercial biobank. We provide a networking platform connecting hospitals and laboratories on the one side, and researchers on the other side. We are supported by Kerala Startup Mission and IIM Kozhikode LIVE. We are currently incubating at the KRIBS-BIONEST Cochin and MSMF Bangalore. Our Institutional Ethics Committee (IEC) is registered with the Ministry of Health, Central Drugs Standard Control Organization. We are also registered with the following Government of India bodies – Ministry of Commerce & Industry - Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade, Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises – Udyam Certification etc.
